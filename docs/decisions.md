# OperaCore — Decision Log

This file tracks key project decisions and why they were made.

---

## 2026-05-31 — Brand name

**Decision:** Use **OperaCore** as the brand name.

**Reason:**
- Communicates operational core, structure, and control.
- Strong enough for a serious automation/process business.
- Works for local businesses and future broader services.

**Risk:**
- `operacore.com` was not available.

**Resolution:**
- Use `operacoresolutions.com` as the main domain.
- Keep the visible brand as **OperaCore**.

---

## 2026-05-31 — Domain

**Decision:** Register `operacoresolutions.com`.

**Reason:**
- Available at low cost.
- Professional `.com` domain.
- Clearer for business customers than a purely abstract name.
- Avoided wasting time searching for a perfect domain.

---

## 2026-05-31 — Registrar / DNS

**Decision:** Use Cloudflare.

**Reason:**
- Good DNS management.
- Email Routing available.
- Strong long-term technical fit.
- Avoids unnecessary hosting or VPS purchases at this stage.

---

## 2026-05-31 — VPS decision

**Decision:** Do not buy a VPS yet.

**Reason:**
- No paying client yet.
- Landing page can run on Netlify/Vercel/Cloudflare Pages.
- Supabase Free and self-hosted n8n are enough for the first demo.
- Avoid technical overbuilding before sales.

**Future trigger:**
- Buy VPS only after first paying pilot or when uptime/public workflows require it.

---

## 2026-06-01 — Account strategy

**Decision:**
- GitHub: use founder's existing GitHub account.
- Netlify: use founder's existing Netlify account.
- Cloudflare: already uses founder's personal Gmail.
- Dedicated Gmail created for OperaCore operational/business accounts: `operacoresolutions@gmail.com`.

**Reason:**
- OperaCore starts as a GitHub-born project.
- Avoid unnecessary account sprawl.
- Keep future business tools separated where useful.

---

## 2026-06-01 — Branding depth

**Decision:** Stop logo refinement after logo pack v2.

**Reason:**
- Assets are good enough to move forward.
- Further refinement risks becoming branding procrastination.
- Priority shifts to repo, documentation, landing page, MVP, and outreach.

**Future improvement:**
- Create vector SVG versions once the project has a stronger public presence or client traction.
